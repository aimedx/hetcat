About

About the Workshop
This workshop is designed to explore the evolving role of artificial intelligence (AI) for accelerating scientific exploration and discovery in heterogeneous catalysis. The event will bring together experts and enthusiasts from various disciplines to discuss the latest advancements, applications, and future potential of AI in catalysis.
